Tacos

A Traditional Mexican Dish for All Occasions

A modern household classic, this tacos recipes is sure to delight both cook and guest alike!

With a quick an easy recipe and minimal cleanup, tacos are perfect for a casual night with friends.

Ingredients

  • Minced meat
  • Grated cheese
  • Taco shells
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. Remove the minced meat from its packaging and cook on a pan for approximately 10 minutes at medium heat
  2. As the meat cooks,prepare the taco shells for use and remove the sourcream and grated cheese from their packaging
  3. When the meat is fully cooked through, divide it into 20g portions, each going into a separate taco shell, before sprinkling a generous helping of cheese on top, followed by a tablespoon of sourcream
  4. For added flavour, add alternative tomato or guacamole dips on the side for selection!
